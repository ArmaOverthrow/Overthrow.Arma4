Install the "{}\+Arma Reforger Tools"{} in Steam. Find it by switching from "{}\+Games"{} to "{}\+Tools"{} in your Steam library.

\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Double click on the Overthrow mod to open it in the Workbench
